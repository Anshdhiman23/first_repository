ABOUT
GGSIPU
EDC

The GGS Indraprastha University-East Campus is committed to providing students with a world-class learning experience, fostering their holistic development. This exemplary campus seamlessly blends aesthetics and technology. As India embraces the fourth Industrial Revolution, the university has taken proactive steps by establishing two new Schools of Studies at the East Campus in Surajmal Vihar, New Delhi.

COURSES
OFFERED

USAR – University School of Automation & Robotics (focuses on AI, Data Science, Machine Learning, IoT, Robotics)
USDI – University School of Design & Innovation (offers B.Design with modern studios)
USAP – University School of Architecture & Planning (moved here in 2023–24; offers B.Arch, M.Arch, M.Plan, Ph.D.)
USMC – University School of Mass Communication (shifted to EDC in 2024–25)

OUR
AIM

The aim of GGSIPU East Delhi Campus (EDC) is essentially to be a modern hub for innovation, entrepreneurship, and multidisciplinary learning while offering students cutting-edge facilities and industry exposure. From the university’s own vision and activities, the aims can be summed up as:
1. Provide World-Class Education:- Host specialized schools (Automation & Robotics, Design & Innovation, Architecture & Planning, Mass Communication) with updated curricula aligned to modern industry demands.
2. Foster Innovation & Startups:- Support student entrepreneurs through the ₹5 crore Innovation & Incubation Fund, mentoring, prototyping labs, and collaboration with IPU’s Innovation Incubation Foundation (IIF) and Atal Incubation Centre (AIC).
3. Create a Multidisciplinary Learning Environment:- Bring together diverse fields — technology, design, architecture, media — on one campus to promote cross-disciplinary collaboration.
4. Enhance Industry Collaboration & Employability:- Build partnerships with companies, run student chapters like IEEE/ACM/AWS/GDG, and conduct skill-building workshops for better placement opportunities.
5. Encourage Social Responsibility & Sustainability:- Promote community engagement through drives, environmental initiatives, and socially impactful projects.
6. Offer State-of-the-Art Campus Life:- Provide an engaging campus experience with modern labs, design studios, sports arenas, auditoriums, and green architecture principles.
